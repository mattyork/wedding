What to Expect

NatureBridge is a kids camp, and we hope to keep that summer camp feeling. There are fire pits, fields, an amphitheater, hiking trails, and canoes. Bring your toys. Expect to play.

We'll be spending a lot of time outside. Along with your fancy wedding clothes, bring a swimsuit, rain gear, and clothes to get dirty in.

Sleeping

You can stay at a cabin on site at no charge. The cabins are fairly rustic - think summer camp. They have twin size bunk beds with camp mattresses, and most rooms also have one double bed. Beds come with linens (sheets and comforter), though you should bring your own pillow. Each cabin has a communal bathroom with multiple stalls and showers. All cabins have electricity. We're staying on site, and we hope you do, too.

If rustic isn't your thing, AirBnB has many listings in the area. You may also be able to snag a room at the Lake Crescent Lodge, a quarter mile from the venue. The website may say they are booked, but they have soom rooms that they save for over-the-phone reservations. Try calling.
